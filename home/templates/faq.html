Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I get a customized system versus working with a platform such as SquareSpace, WordPress or even Shopify?

+

The reason we believe in our services and end-product versus these other platforms, is because eventually you will find it very difficult to grow your online business within the constraints of their platforms. Here at Prairie Code we do not partner with platforms to build our services. We utilize fundamental web technologies that allow us to build out your system and scale it. Ask yourself, which truly succesful businesses are constrained within a shopify or WordPress platform?

What can you do for my business?

+

Here at Prairie Code, we first ask you for your key business insights, we find that the input that you provide for us is crucial for the delivery of a succesful system. We take the requirements gathered on your end, as well as what we feel your online system could benefit from. The end result is a fully-customized and sophisticated system that bolsters your businesses online presence.

What can we expect from a delivered product of yours?

+

When it is all said and done, you can expect an online system fully customized and suited to meet your exact needs. The cost is a one-time only for our services to develop the system. (Yearly maintenance fees are an option) Your website will be fully-secured using the latest security measures, and your domain will be registered through a DNS. We will leverage cloud technologies in order to avoid hardware overhead, the more busier your site gets the more cloud resources we can leverage as well.

How do we contact Prairie Code for maintenance related tasks?

+

In order to contact us for maintenance related tasks, you will be given a login at your project launch. Whenever you have an issue on your website, you can login to your account, and through our ticketing portal you will be able to upload a ticket for maintenance related tasks.

How does payment look like?

+

At Prairie Code we only ask for payment at the end, when the product is to be launched, if your system would require several months of substantial amounts of development, we would need to negotiate a quarterly or weekly payment plan along with a security deposit. Check or cash is our preferred method of payment.
